## 4. Spaces & Zones

### 4.1 The Euphoria — Ceremonious Hall
The grand centrepiece of the venue. A large, fully air-conditioned ceremonious hall that can accommodate up to **1200 guests**. Perfect for the main wedding ceremony, reception, or any large-scale event. It can be combined with The Tavern and the Serene Courtyard to create a seamless flow across indoor and outdoor spaces.

### 4.2 Serene Courtyard — Landscaped Lawn
A stunning outdoor lawn with lush green landscaping, a beautiful waterfall, and a romantic entry pathway. Ideal for contemporary-style outdoor weddings, ceremonies under the stars, and outdoor celebrations. Capacity: **800 guests seated**. Directly connected to The Tavern (AC hall) — so rainy days are never a worry. Rated #1 outdoor space at the venue.

### 4.3 The Tavern — Dining Area
A fully air-conditioned hall of **4,000 sq ft** overlooking the Serene Courtyard and waterfall. Used for wedding receptions, dining, leisure conferencing, and private parties. Capacity: **200 guests per sitting**. Has its own private entry — perfect for smaller, intimate gatherings. Can be combined with The Euphoria and Serene Courtyard for larger events.

### 4.4 The Sundeck — Patio
An intimate outdoor patio space overlooking the lush green lawns of the Serene Courtyard and the surrounding natural beauty. Perfect for intimate gatherings, romantic candle-light dinners, high tea events, and small private celebrations with close family and friends.

### 4.5 The Azure — Open Deck
A breathtaking, dramatic **4,000 sq ft** open private deck space. Comes with **3 luxurious suites attached** to it — ideal for an intimate group of close family and friends. Perfect for sangeet ceremonies, private parties, cocktail evenings, and weekend getaways. Guarantees a wow factor.

### 4.6 Luxurious Guest Rooms
**15 plush, elegantly furnished guest rooms**, each accommodating up to 4–5 guests comfortably (total ~70 guests). Rooms feature:
- Modern amenities and décor
- Wi-Fi connectivity
- Private terrace access
- Available as part of events or for standalone weekend getaways

### 4.7 Bridal Suite
A stunning **7-star bridal suite** — exclusively designed for the bride's preparations and comfort on her special day.

### 4.8 Terrace Area
Additional open terrace space available for events, offering stunning views of the surrounding greenery and city skyline.
